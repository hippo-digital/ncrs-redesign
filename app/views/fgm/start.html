About this prototype

This prototype explores how we could introduce the FGM indicator and add and remove functionality to the redesigned SCRa.

A FGM indicator will be added to SCRa to inform health workers, with the relevant access, of any information relating to patients that have a family history of FGM. If a health worker has knowledge of FGM in relation to a patient then they can either add or remove the FGM indicator as appropriate.
