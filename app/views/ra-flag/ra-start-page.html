Create a Reasonable Adjustments record

Use this feature to add a Reasonable Adjustments record for a patient.

Before you start

Please ensure it is appropriate for the patient to have Reasonable Adjustments before creating a record.

The patient must have “a physical or mental impairment which has a substantial and long-term adverse effect on their ability to carry out normal day-to-day activities” (the definition of disability as defined by the Equality Act (2010) (opens in a new tab)).

You will be asked to provide details of:

  • how consent has been obtained
  • the patient's impairments (the patient may prefer not to say) (optional)
  • the patient's required Reasonable Adjustments (optional)
